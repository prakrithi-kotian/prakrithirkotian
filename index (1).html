Welcome to RNSIT

RNS Institute of Technology (RNSIT), Bengaluru, is one of the premier engineering institutions in Karnataka, renowned for its academic excellence, research, and innovation. Affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and recognized as an autonomous institute, RNSIT offers a vibrant campus life, accomplished faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.

About RNSIT

Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical education and research, fostering professionals with ethical values and serving society.
